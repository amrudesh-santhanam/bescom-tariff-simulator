# BESCOM Electricity Tariff Summary for Factories

This document outlines the billing structure for industrial consumers under Karnataka's BESCOM regulations, specifically for LT (Low Tension) and HT (High Tension) industrial categories.

## 1. How Factories Pay
Factories are categorized based on their supply type and size:

* **Low Tension (LT-5):** For smaller factories with a requisitioned load less than 150 kW / 201 HP. These are billed based on their connected kilowatts (kW).
* **High Tension (HT-2(a)):** For larger factories with a contract demand of 50 kW / 67 HP and above. These are billed based on kilovolt-amperes (kVA) of billing demand.

---

## 2. Fixed Charges
Fixed charges are the baseline monthly fees paid by consumers based on their sanctioned load or horsepower (HP), regardless of actual electricity consumption.

* **For LT Industrial Units (Standard LT-5):**
    * **Below 100 HP:** Rs. 140 per kW per month.
    * **100 HP and above:** Rs. 250 per kW per month.

---

## 3. Demand Charges
Demand charges apply if a factory opts for a **Demand-Based Tariff** (optional for LT, mandatory for HT). Instead of paying for sanctioned capacity, charges are calculated based on the *actual peak usage* (Maximum Demand) recorded by a Tri-Vector meter during the month.

* **For LT Industrial Units (Optional Demand-Based LT-5):**
    * **Below 100 HP:** Rs. 190 per kW per month.
    * **100 HP and above:** Rs. 300 per kW per month.
    * *Rule:* Billing demand is the sanctioned load or the Maximum Demand (MD) recorded, whichever is higher. If MD exceeds sanctioned load, a penalty of two times the normal rate applies.

* **For HT Industrial Units (HT-2(a)):**
    * **Standard Rate:** Rs. 340 per kVA of billing demand per month.
    * *Rule:* Billing demand is the maximum demand recorded during the month or 90% of the Contract Demand (CD), whichever is higher. If it exceeds CD, deterrent charges are levied at two times the normal rate.

---

## 4. Usage (Energy) Charges
Usage charges are variable costs paid for the actual volume of electricity consumed, measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh).

* **LT Industrial Units (LT-5):** 610 paise per unit for the entire consumption.
* **HT Industrial Units (HT-2(a)):** 690 paise per unit for the entire consumption.

---

## 5. Time of Day (ToD) Tariff Note
Factories may be subject to ToD systems. 
* **Non-Monsoon Period (Dec–June):**
    * **Peak Hours (06:00–09:00 & 18:00–22:00):** Penalty surcharge of (+) 100 paise per unit.
    * **Off-Peak Hours (22:00–06:00):** Rebate incentive of (-) 100 paise per unit.
